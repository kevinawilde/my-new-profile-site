About Me

I was born August 7th, 1991 in Snoqualmie Washington. I moved to the state of Wisconsin when I was 8 years-old. From the time that I turned 17, I have moved several different times, and my adventures have taken me all over the world! While my travels have taken me as far as Norway, my home base is in Austin, Texas. I have been married for nearly two years to the most beautiful woman in the world! I am fluently bilingual, speaking English and Spanish, thanks to a two-year church mission that I served in Mexico! While my original plan was to become a physical therapist, I could not contain my inner geek and now I am pursuing Software Development!
